Tax-saving products & conditions

For any questions about tax-saving / asset-building accounts and their
benefits, eligibility, income/asset requirements, age requirements, and tax
treatment, refer to TAX_SAVING.md.

This covers: 연금저축펀드 (pension savings fund), IRP, ISA, 해외주식 직접투자
(overseas stock direct investment / capital gains tax), 강화된 ISA (proposed
"super ISA" / domestic-investment ISA), RIA 계좌 (Reshoring Investment
Account), and 청년미래적금 (Youth Future Savings).

Note: figures and rules are as of June 2026 and change over time. The 강화된
ISA section is a pending proposal, not enacted law — flag this when relevant.
